Getting Started with your Brightcove Campaign™ Trial

{{ page.description }}

Brightcove Campaign is designed for demand generation marketers and makes it easy to configure, publish, and measure video performance within campaigns.

Sign up for a trial account

Brightcove offers Brightcove Campaign trial accounts, sign up here.

Trial accounts have the following limitations:

  • A maximum of five videos can be uploaded to an account. If you need to be able to upload more than 5 videos while the account is in trial mode, contact our sales team
  • A maximum of 3 users can be created
  • Only 1 connection to a marketing automation platform can be created
